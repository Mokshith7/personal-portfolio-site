---
title: "Day 3 — The days I long for"
excerpt: "A small poem about childhood — the simple joys we didn't know we were living for the last time."
category: "daily-scribbles"
date: "2026-03-26"
---

I long for the days when march was filled with anticipation of summer vacation.

I long for the days when me and my friends played in scorching sun and finding cold rasna when we came back.

I long for the days when my grandma used to give 5 rupees and we would run to buy our favourite lays packet.

I long for the days when it rained so heavily and the school shut down, cozily wrapped up in my blanket watching Doraemon.

I long for the days when there was a powercut in the night and the entire neighbourhood used to come out and talk.

I long for the days when I used to sit next to my best friend and talk while trying to avoid the eagle like gaze of our teachers.

I long for the days when I came back home and mom would feed me food with her own hands.

I long for the days when the only villain in my life was my sister and the harmless fights.

I long for those raw mangoes, those kulfis, those childhood games and those friends.

I long for the days when I still had a spark in me.
