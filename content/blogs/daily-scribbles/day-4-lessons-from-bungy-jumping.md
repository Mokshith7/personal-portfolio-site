Recently I jumped from India's tallest bungy jumping tower. It was an unnerving experience. But it taught me a few things too.

**Lesson 1: Always take deep breaths before doing something big or scary.**

My guru has taught me the anuloma viloma pranayama and she said it will come handy when you need it. Before jumping I took deep breaths and realised how effective they were.

**Lesson 2: Trust the universe.**

In bungy jump there is a cable attached to your waist that will not let you fall. Likewise universe too has your back. Universe will not let you fall. Trust.

**Lesson 3: Have fun!**

Take the dive and enjoy the experience. Life too is an adventure and when you take it too seriously you miss out on the fun. Just take a leap and enjoy every experience along the way.
