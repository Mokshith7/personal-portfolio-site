# My First Trip to Japan

Japan has always been on my bucket list, and this trip exceeded all expectations.

## Highlights

### Kyoto
The ancient capital stole my heart with its beautiful temples and traditional geisha districts. Walking through Arashiyama bamboo grove at sunrise was magical.

### Tokyo
The energy of Tokyo is unmatched. From the famous Shibuya crossing to the serene Meiji Shrine, every corner offers something new.

## What I Learned

Japan taught me the beauty of respect—for traditions, for others, and for the environment. The attention to detail in everything, from food presentation to train schedules, was inspiring.
