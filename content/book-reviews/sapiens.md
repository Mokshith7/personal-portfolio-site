# Sapiens by Yuval Noah Harari

A thought-provoking journey through the history of our species.

## What I Loved

Harari has a unique way of making complex historical and anthropological concepts accessible. His three revolutions framework—Cognitive, Agricultural, and Scientific—provides a powerful lens for understanding human development.

## Key Insights

- The power of shared myths and stories in building civilizations
- How the Agricultural Revolution was both a blessing and a curse
- The role of money, empires, and religion in unifying humanity

## My Takeaway

This book changed how I think about human progress and our place in history.
