# The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

A timeless tale about a shepherd boy's journey to find treasure.

## The Story

Santiago, a young shepherd, dreams of a treasure hidden near the Egyptian pyramids. His journey to find it teaches him about the importance of following one's Personal Legend.

## Memorable Quotes

> "When you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it."

> "The secret of life is to fall seven times and to get up eight times."

## Why I Recommend It

This book is a gentle reminder to pursue our dreams and trust the journey.
