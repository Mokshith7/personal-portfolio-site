Finally took the plunge and bought an acoustic guitar!

## The Guitar

I went with a beginner-friendly acoustic guitar. Nothing too expensive, but the sound is warm and inviting.

## Learning to Hold It

Spent the first hour just learning how to hold it correctly. Posture matters more than I thought.

## First Chord: E Minor

E minor - my very first chord! It only uses two fingers, making it perfect for beginners. My fingers hurt after just 10 minutes, but I managed to get a clean sound a few times.

## Tonight's Goal

Practice transitioning between E minor and A minor. These two chords are supposedly easy to switch between for beginners.

The journey of a thousand songs begins with a single chord!
