Today was all about understanding how colors work together.

## Color Theory Basics

I spent the morning watching tutorials about color theory. Primary colors (red, yellow, blue) can create all other colors - at least in theory!

## My Color Wheel

I created my first color wheel. It's not perfect, but it helped me understand:
- Complementary colors (opposites on the wheel)
- Analogous colors (neighbors on the wheel)
- Warm vs cool colors

## Experiments

I mixed some unexpected combinations and was surprised by the results. A touch of purple in orange creates this beautiful earthy tone that I'm now obsessed with.

## Reflection

Color is so much more complex than I thought. The same red can look completely different next to yellow versus blue. Context changes everything - just like in life.
